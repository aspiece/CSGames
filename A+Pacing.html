CompTIA A+ Pacing Guide (16 Days)

Each day is a 1.5 hour session. Each agenda follows Merrill’s Effective Instruction (Activation → Demonstration → Application → Integration). All worksheets, videos, and assessments are distributed via Google Classroom. The substitute will use a slide deck (projected) to pace class time and display discussion prompts, timers, and brain-break instructions.

Click a day number to reveal that day's plan. Sub materials and all student documents live in Google Classroom; slides are used to project any prompts or timers.

1 Intro to Troubleshooting 7-step method, error messages
  • Activation (15m): Students share personal tech problem stories (slide with prompts).
  • Demonstration (30m): Play posted video from Google Classroom; sub facilitates discussion using projected slide questions.
  • Application (25m): Hands-on troubleshooting on provided VMs (lab manager assists).
  • Integration (10m): Volunteers share fixes; class discussion guided by slides.
  • Formative: Quick exit activity submitted in Google Classroom.
 Stretch & Chat: Project slide with 3 stretches (arms up, touch toes, neck rolls), then a single prompt for each student to share a favorite hobby. Timer on slide (5 minutes). — Play video from Classroom, monitor VM activity, prompt students to submit exit activity in Classroom. Post video and exit activity to Google Classroom. Add stretch & chat slide and activation prompts to the slide deck.
2 Frozen Systems & BSODs App freezes, blue screens
  • Activation (15m): Show screenshot on slide; class suggests steps.
  • Demonstration (20m): Play Classroom video; project discussion prompts.
  • Application (35m): Diagnose BSODs in VMs or case scenarios (lab manager assists).
  • Integration (10m): Group share guided by slides.
  • Formative: Short quiz/response in Google Classroom.
 Riddle Challenge: Project slide with 2 riddles (egg, candle). Read, allow guesses, reveal answers on slide. Timer on slide (5 minutes). — Show screenshot from slide, run Classroom quiz, monitor student submissions. Post video and Classroom quiz. Add screenshots, riddles, and answer slides to the slide deck.
3 Boot Failures & Hardware Issues Boot device errors, PSU/RAM, BIOS
  • Activation (15m): Play POST beep audio (hosted in Classroom); students guess causes via slide prompts.
  • Demonstration (20m): Classroom video + projected explanation slides.
  • Application (35m): BIOS boot order lab in VMs or guided checklist in Classroom.
  • Integration (10m): Short share-out using slide prompts.
  • Formative: Reflection submitted in Classroom.
 Would You Rather: Project slide with 3 scenarios. Tally via show of hands; result slide. Timer (5 minutes). Checkpoint Quiz #1 (posted to Classroom) Play POST beep audio from Classroom, guide BIOS activity, collect reflection and quiz submissions in Classroom. Post video, beep audio, BIOS lab guide and checkpoint quiz to Classroom. Add Would You Rather slides and tally instructions to deck.
